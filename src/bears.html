Bears

Yellowstone and Grand Teton

The American Black Bear is the most common bear in North America. Black Bear is the most timid and least dangerous bears. Not all black bear have black fur. Their fur color can range from black to light brown. The grizzly bear and brown bear are members of the same species of bear. While most grizzlies live in Alaska and Northwestern Canada, they are also found in the lower 48 in Montana, Yellowstone, and Northern Utah. The best identifier for a grizzly is the large hump on their shoulders, best seen while walking.

Antlers

Yellowstone and Antelope Island

Antlers are usually only found on males with the exception of reindeer/caribou. While antlers grow, they are usually covered in highly vascular skin, called velvet. They are bone and grow faster than any other mammal bone. Most antler shedding is annual. Although the antlers are regrown each year, their size varies with the age of the animal.

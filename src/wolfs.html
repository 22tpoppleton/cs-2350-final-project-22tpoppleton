Wovles and Coyotes

Yellowstone

The wolf is a large canine native to Eurasia and North America. Wolves travel in nuclear families and is the most specialized in cooperative game hunting. They have a highly advanced expressive behavior, including individual or group howling. Coyotes are closely related to wolves, although much smaller. Coyotes are native to Northern America. Coyotes are highly flexible in social organization, living either in a family unit or loosely knit packs of unrelated individuals.
